A hybrid farming simulation and tower defense game that combines peaceful agricultural management with intense tactical combat in a unique day/night cycle.

## üìñ About

Seeds of Success was a game development prototype that blended two distinct gaming experiences into one cohesive gameplay loop. Players took on the role of a survivor rebuilding their community after an apocalyptic event, managing their farm during the day while defending it against demonic invasions at night.

### Core Concept

- **Day Phase (12 minutes)**: Focus on farming, crafting, exploration, and community building
- **Night Phase (8 minutes)**: Defend your farm against waves of enemies in tactical tower defense combat
- **Interconnected Systems**: Crops grown during the day fuel combat abilities at night, while combat victories unlock new agricultural opportunities

- **Animal husbandry**: Chickens, cows, sheep, pigs, fish, and ducks with unique productions
- **Quality system**: Normal, Rare, Epic, and Legendary crop qualities
